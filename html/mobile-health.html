Mobile Health & Wellness App

A cross-platform mobile application empowering users to track fitness goals, monitor health metrics, and connect with healthcare professionals — all from a single intuitive interface.

Technology Stack

React Native TypeScript Node.js Firebase AWS Redux PostgreSQL Swift Kotlin GraphQL

Key Features

Real-time health metrics tracking and monitoring
Personalized fitness goal setting and progress tracking
In-app video consultations with healthcare professionals
Wearable device integration (Apple Watch, Fitbit, Garmin)
Nutrition logging and calorie tracking with food database
Smart push notifications and medication reminders
HIPAA-compliant secure health data storage
AI-driven wellness insights and weekly health reports
